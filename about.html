About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

Foster is a new e-learning platform focused on STEM subjects for K12 and further education sectors. The platform also supports an under-resourced teaching profession by providing them with engaging content to support the curriculum. Foster will go beyond traditional MOOC and LMS platforms by providing STEM Kits delivered using Augmented Reality (AG) and Virtual Reality (VR).
